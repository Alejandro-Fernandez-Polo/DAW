About

About me

Hello! I'm Alejandro, the founder and sole graphic designer behind Reliance, a creative studio dedicated to delivering impactful, personalized visual solutions.

My history

Since my beginning in the world of graphic design, I have always believed in the power of creativity to transform ideas into memorable visual experiences. After years of experience working on various projects, I decided to establish Reliance with the aim of providing design services that go beyond the conventional.

Design Philosophy

At Reliance, we are passionate about fusing visual aesthetics with functionality. We believe that each project is an opportunity to tell a unique story and to express our client's identity through graphic design. Our dedicated team strives for innovation and excellence in every aspect of our work.

Our focus

We specialize in logo design, branding, web design, and are proud to offer custom solutions that fit each client's specific needs. We work closely with our clients, collaborating at every stage of the process to ensure exceptional results.

RAIJUU

RAIJUU Pets to You is a pet food store that offers nutritional and high-quality products for pets. The name “RAIJUU” means “thunder beast” in Japanese and represents the energy, strength, and loyalty of pets. The logo is bright yellow and shaped like an animal. The store's goal is to provide healthy and tasty food that is loved by both pets and their owners. This project presents the store details, product catalog, marketing plan, and business strategy.
