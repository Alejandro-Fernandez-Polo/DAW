How to come
Why to come

To come here is to experience the geographic vastness, from the lights of New York to the breathtaking scenery of the Grand Canyon. Immerse yourself in the eclectic culture, from local festivals to culinary diversity. The opportunities for adventure are endless, whether exploring national parks or discovering history at iconic landmarks. In addition, the friendliness of the people and constant innovation create a unique experience. Come to the United States to discover the richness of its land, its people and its unwavering spirit.

By Air
Efficiently access the United States through air travel, leveraging major international airports in cities like New York, Boston, Chicago, and San Francisco. Daily seamless flights make air travel the optimal choice. Complying with baggage restrictions and carrying necessary documents ensures a smooth customs and immigration process. Despite the absence of direct flights from India, connecting flights offer the fastest route. For instance, Mumbai connections may include Newark Liberty and San Francisco, Bangalore flights may stop in Dubai en route to Atlanta, and Delhi travelers can opt for flights to Canada with stopovers in Vienna and London, followed by a scenic road trip to the USA.
By Sea
Private boats are a niche transport mode favored by European tourists and domestic travelers exploring the U.S. via ferries, cruises, and boats. The primary ports, situated in Los Angeles and Florida, serve as gateways for tourists, facilitating convenient transitions to car rentals, cabs, or trains for onward travel across America. Although less prevalent today, these facilities endure, catering to enthusiasts interested in fishing or boat owners seeking leisure rides. While not a mainstream choice, this mode of transport presents a distinctive and leisurely option for those looking to navigate the diverse landscapes and waterways that define the United States.
By Train
You can travel to other cities in America via Train or tourists traveling from Canada or New Mexico can use the train to reach America. Amtrak is the only operator that provides train facilities from Canada to America and the other way round. One can board an Amtrak Train from either Toronto, Montreal or Vancouver located in Canada to reach the USA. If you are traveling from Mexico, they need to come to Canada by bus or car and then board a train from the above-mentioned places. You can travel from city to city within America via train in a very Swift and smooth manner. This mode of transport is cheaper, faster and more comfortable as one can freely move around on a train.
By Road
Embark on an unforgettable road trip to explore America, a journey accessible for both Canadian residents and travelers. Ensure you carry passports and are ready to pay a $6 entrance fee when crossing borders. The US-Canada and US-Mexico borders are frequently crossed, with the latter requiring extra caution due to stringent surveillance. Cooperation with officers is crucial, given concerns about drug trafficking. Despite potential checks, road tripping through cities like Los Angeles, Seattle, New York, Las Vegas, and Boston offers a unique experience with scenic views. America's well-connected highway network makes car travel the fastest and most comfortable mode, provided speed limits are observed.
