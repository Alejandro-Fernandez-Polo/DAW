Team
Main chef
Juan Pedro Ortiz

Juan P. Ortiz, a virtuoso chef from Seville, forged his culinary passion in a catering family. After his training at Estimar and Restaurante La Costa, guided by masters such as José Álvarez and Quique Dacosta, he shone in outstanding kitchens in Seville and Mallorca. His culinary journey took him to Ovo in Granada, where as chef and head chef, he masterfully fuses the rich Andalusian tradition with innovative techniques. Each dish he creates tells the story of his experience and dedication, consolidating him as a reference in the gastronomic scene, elevating Granada cuisine to new heights.

Room Manager
Lidia Outeda

Lidia Outeda, Ovo's charismatic head waitress, is a Sevillian with deep roots in hospitality. Born into a hospitality family, from an early age she has had an exceptional appreciation for gastronomy. Her career includes experiences in the best restaurants in Seville and Mallorca, where she perfected her art under diverse culinary influences. She decided to move to Granada to join the exciting project of Ovo en Faralá, where day by day, with sympathy and mastery, she elevates the experience of diners. Her constant training and personal charm make her an essential figure for the welcoming essence of Ovo.

General Manager
Javier Feixas

Javier Feixas, formerly the head chef at Ovo, has masterfully assumed the role of General Manager following the arrival of Juan Pedro Ortiz as the new chef. With a career marked by culinary excellence, Javier has demonstrated exceptional versatility as he now leads the overall management of the restaurant. His transition highlights his deep understanding of both the kitchen and operational aspects of the business. Javier, with his experience and passion, plays a key role in ensuring that Ovo's vision and quality are maintained in all aspects, from the kitchen to the overall customer experience.
