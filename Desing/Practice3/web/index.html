Welcome to ovo

A gastronomic oasis in the heart of Granada!

Since 1976, Ovo has been the place where Andalusian culinary tradition meets bold innovation. Recently, we have undergone a complete transformation, renovating our space to bring to life a minimalist style that reflects our passion for modern elegance.

At Ovo, we don't just offer food; we offer an experience. Each dish tells a story, a fusion of authentic flavors and cutting-edge culinary techniques, courtesy of our talented chef Juan Morales and his passionate team.

Our menu is a journey through Granada's rich gastronomic history, reinvented with a contemporary twist. From classics like salmorejo to unique creations that defy expectations, every bite is a celebration of culinary excellence.

Recently awarded a Michelin star, we are excited to share our passion for food with you. At Ovo, you won't just find a restaurant; you'll discover a corner where tradition and modernity dance in harmony, where every visit is a memorable experience.

Join us on this unique culinary journey - welcome to Ovo, where history meets the Michelin star!

History

In 1976, a gastronomic corner called Ovo emerged in the heart of Granada. For more than four decades, this establishment became a benchmark for Andalusian cuisine, fusing tradition and avant-garde to offer a unique culinary experience in the region.

The year 2021 marked a crucial chapter in Ovo's history. The family owners, determined to take the restaurant to new heights, undertook an ambitious renovation. The old premises, steeped in nostalgia, underwent a complete metamorphosis, adopting a minimalist and elegant style. Ancient stone walls met polished surfaces and a soft color palette, creating an environment that reflects the essence of Granada in a contemporary context.

This transformation was not limited to design; It also extended to the letter. Chef Juan Morales led a passionate team in reinterpreting the classics of Andalusian cuisine. Presenting a unique fusion of authentic flavors and artistic presentations.

The result did not take long to garner recognition. Ovo became a source of pride for Granada and attracted the attention of renowned gastronomic critics. The climax came with obtaining a Michelin star, a distinction that consecrated culinary excellence and the effort dedicated to the fusion of tradition with modernity.

Today, Ovo is not just a restaurant; It is a gastronomic destination where each dish tells a story, where the history of Granada is intertwined with contemporary culinary creativity. A place where the Michelin star shines as testimony to an exceptional gastronomic experience.

A restaurant with personality

Juan P Ortiz, chef and head chef, after years under the orders of great references of the national cuisine in Estimar, Restaurante La Costa, of José Álvarez or Deesa of Quique Dacosta, presents his culinary project from the kitchens of Epicúreo to project the cuisine of Granada to the place it deserves in the gastronomic panorama.

Lidia Outeda, our head waitress, is from Seville, daughter of restaurateurs, and has had the good fortune to appreciate gastronomy from a very young age as few others know how to do. In constant training, and having worked in some of the best restaurants in Seville and Mallorca, she decided to come to Granada, to be part of the exciting project that is Epicúreo, and in which, day by day, with his sympathy and good work, elevates the experience of our guests.

At Ovo, the culinary experience is a symphony of flavors, where Andalusian tradition meets innovation. Under the direction of Chef Juan P. Ortiz, each dish is a masterpiece that celebrates local ingredients with avant-garde culinary techniques. The minimalist and elegant setting complements the excellence on the plate, creating the perfect setting for an unforgettable evening. Backed by a Michelin star and a passionate team, Ovo not only offers exceptional food, but also a unique gastronomic narrative that takes diners on a journey of culinary delight in the heart of Granada.
